Aims

  • To introduce some of the main concepts in Finite Geometry.
  • To increase the audiences knowledge of the many results and conjectures in Finite Geometry.
  • To provide an opportunity for PhD students to discuss and share their own projects.
  • To enable mathematicians in this area to meet and plan scientiﬁc collaboration.

Registration

    The registration fee is 100 pounds. (Please note that the registration fee does not cover accommodation.)
    The registration fee includes the welcome reception and coffee break.
    To complete your registration you need to make your payments (registration fee + campus accommodation).
    Deadline for registration: Friday 12 May, 2017!
To register follow the link: Registration


Contributed Talks

    Research students are invited to give a 15 minute talk on their work.
    Please write your abstract using our template abstract.tex and email both LaTeX and PDF files to fingeo@sussex.ac.uk.
    Please name the files and emails as Surname_Firstname_Abstract.
    Deadline for submitting the abstract: Monday 15 May, 2017!

Accommodation

    We will make reservations for campus accommodation. You do not need to make any further arrangements if you want to stay on campus.
    We will be offering single occupancy rooms only.
    The rooms will be offered in Stanmer Court (ensuite rooms), Park Village and East Slope.
    The price has not been finalised for next year yet but a rough estimate for educational visitors is:
  • Stanmer Court (Ensuite room): £27/night
  • Park Village (Shared bathroom): £22/night
  • East Slope (Shared bathroom): £22/night
